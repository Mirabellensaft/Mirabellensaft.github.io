Lines & Control

A Practice. 2024/10/17

Don't hold the brush like a ball point pen.
Hold it right at its end.
Hold it with two fingers.
Hold it away from your body.


Draw a line.
Draw it, where it goes.
Let go of controlling it.
Only to gain it again, to let it go.


Let the control flow.
Back and forth.
